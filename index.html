Welcome to HealMates

Three-quarters of all suicides are men, with suicide being the leading cause of death for men under 50 in many countries, including the UK.

Despite these alarming figures, many men continue to suffer in silence, believing that seeking help is a sign of weakness.

At HealMates, we aim to change this narrative by offering a safe space for men to talk openly about their struggles and find the support they need to reclaim their mental wellbeing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is mental health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act.

How can I improve my mental health?

Improving mental health can involve regular exercise, a healthy diet, adequate sleep, and seeking professional help when needed.
