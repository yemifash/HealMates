About Us

HealMates is dedicated to advancing men's mental health and wellbeing. Our goal is to help men feel more connected, supported, and knowledgeable about their options.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide a safe and supportive environment where men can openly discuss their mental health challenges and find the resources they need to improve their wellbeing.

History and Background

Founded in 2020, HealMates was established to address the growing need for mental health support among men. Our founders recognized the stigma surrounding men's mental health and sought to create a space where men could feel comfortable seeking help.

Impact and Achievements

Since our inception, HealMates has supported over 1,000 men through our programs. 90% of participants report improved mental health and a greater sense of community.

Partnerships and Collaborations

We are proud to collaborate with organizations such as Mental Health America and the National Alliance on Mental Illness to expand our reach and impact.
