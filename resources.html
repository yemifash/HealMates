Helplines

If you or someone you know needs urgent help, please reach out to one of these helplines:

  • Samaritans: 116 123 (UK, Free)
  • CALM Helpline: 0800 58 58 58 (UK, Free)
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-8255 (US)
